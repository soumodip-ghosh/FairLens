# FairLens

## Revolutionizing Fairness in Machine Learning

FairLens is an innovative web application designed to detect and explain biases in machine learning datasets. By leveraging advanced fairness metrics and the power of Google's Gemini AI, FairLens empowers data scientists, researchers, and organizations to build more equitable AI systems.

## What FairLens Does

FairLens analyzes your machine learning datasets to identify potential biases that could lead to unfair outcomes. It calculates key fairness metrics such as demographic parity, disparate impact, and equal opportunity, providing both quantitative measurements and qualitative explanations powered by cutting-edge AI.

## How It Works

1. **Upload Your Data**: Simply upload your CSV dataset through our intuitive web interface.

2. **Select Attributes**: Choose your target prediction variable and sensitive attributes (like gender, race, or age) that you want to monitor for bias.

3. **Automated Analysis**: FairLens trains a quick model on your data and computes fairness metrics across different demographic groups.

4. **AI-Powered Insights**: Get detailed explanations from Gemini AI about what the metrics mean and practical strategies to mitigate any detected biases.

5. **Visual Results**: View your fairness metrics in an easy-to-understand dashboard with color-coded indicators for quick assessment.

## Key Benefits

- **Ethical AI Development**: Ensure your models treat all users fairly, reducing the risk of discriminatory outcomes.

- **Regulatory Compliance**: Help meet emerging AI fairness regulations and ethical guidelines.

- **Improved Model Performance**: Bias detection often reveals data quality issues that improve overall model accuracy.

- **Educational Value**: Learn about fairness concepts through AI-generated explanations tailored to your specific data.

- **Time-Saving**: Automate bias detection that would otherwise require extensive manual analysis.

- **Transparency**: Build trust with stakeholders by demonstrating your commitment to fair AI practices.

## Why FairLens Matters

In an era where AI systems increasingly influence critical decisions in hiring, lending, healthcare, and criminal justice, ensuring fairness is not just an ethical imperative—it's a business necessity. FairLens bridges the gap between complex fairness research and practical application, making bias detection accessible to everyone.

## Features

- **Comprehensive Metrics**: Calculates multiple fairness indicators including demographic parity difference, disparate impact ratio, and equal opportunity difference.

- **Gemini AI Integration**: Leverages Google's latest AI models for intelligent bias explanations and mitigation recommendations.

- **User-Friendly Interface**: Clean, responsive web app built with React and styled with Tailwind CSS.

- **FastAPI Backend**: Robust Python backend for efficient data processing and model training.

- **Real-Time Analysis**: Get results instantly without complex setup or infrastructure.

## Use Cases

- **Hiring Algorithms**: Detect bias in resume screening systems.

- **Credit Scoring**: Ensure lending decisions are fair across demographics.

- **Healthcare AI**: Identify biases in diagnostic or treatment recommendation models.

- **Criminal Justice**: Analyze fairness in risk assessment tools.

- **Academic Research**: Study fairness concepts with real datasets.

## Technology Stack

- **Frontend**: React with Tailwind CSS for modern, responsive UI
- **Backend**: FastAPI with Python for high-performance API
- **AI**: Google Gemini for intelligent explanations
- **ML**: Scikit-learn for fairness metric calculations
- **Data Processing**: Pandas for efficient data handling
